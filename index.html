▶ Screening Criteria

Application Requirements:

  • Completed rental application with all required information
  • Valid government-issued photo ID
  • Proof of income (pay stubs, employment letter, or tax returns)
  • Banking information and statements

Income Requirements:

  • Monthly gross income must be at least 3x the monthly rent
  • Stable employment history (minimum 6 months current position)
  • Verifiable income sources

Credit & Background:

  • Credit score of 650 or higher preferred
  • Background check with no violent criminal history
  • No recent evictions or landlord disputes

References:

  • Previous landlord references (if applicable)
  • Personal references
  • Employment verification
