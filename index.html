▶ Application Process and Screening Criteria

Application Process and Screening Criteria

General Process
  • We offer an application form to everyone

    • We review only fully completed applications in the order received

    We may require up to 5 business days to verify information

    We screen: Financial Background, Criminal Background, Prior Rental History, References

    Complete Application

    Each applicant over 18 must submit an individual application via our website

    We will not review incomplete applications

    Must present government issued photo identification

    We will accept the first qualified applicant(s)

    Financial Background

    Household income must be at least 2.5x monthly rent (non-Portland) or 2-2.5x in Portland

    Co-signers may be required (subject to individual approval)

    Income must be verifiable through pay stubs, employer contact, award letters, tax records, or bank statements

    May require credit report obtained within past 30 days

    Negative credit (late payments, collections, judgments, bankruptcy) may result in denial

    Criminal Background

    Will be denied for convictions including: drug-related crimes, person crimes, sex offenses, financial fraud

    Effective 1/1/2020: Cannot consider marijuana use/possession or medical marijuana status

    Prior Rental History

    No evictions within past five years

    Must provide information to contact past landlords

    References

    Favorable references from current employers, past landlords, and other relevant persons

    Explanations/Exceptions

    Written explanations for extenuating circumstances may be submitted

    Exceptions may be made with increased deposits or qualified co-signers

    Falsification or misrepresentation will result in denial

    Liability Insurance Requirements

    Required: $100,000 coverage in property damage and personal liability

    Must be from an A-rated carrier

    Evidence required prior to occupancy and at lease renewal
